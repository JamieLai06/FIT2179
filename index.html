The Rising Cost of Living
in Malaysia

This visual analytics dashboard explores the evolving Consumer Price Index (CPI) across Malaysia and the world. Through interactive maps, trends, category analysis, and inflation breakdowns, the dashboard reveals how rising living costs reshape spending patterns, household pressure, and economic reality.

Why Cost of Living Matters

Inflation affects more than prices. It changes household behaviour, purchasing power, and financial wellbeing. By examining CPI trends across categories, regions, and income groups, this dashboard highlights where inflation pressure is strongest and how living costs have evolved between 2020 and 2026.

Global Cost of Living Landscape

The global overview compares living costs across countries. High-income economies dominate the upper range, while developing economies generally experience lower living index values.

Malaysia CPI Overview

National CPI trends reveal how inflation accelerated after the pandemic period. Regional differences across Malaysian states also highlight uneven living costs nationwide.

Annual CPI Growth

Malaysia's overall CPI increased steadily from 2016 onward, with stronger inflationary pressure after 2021.

State CPI Distribution

States show varying CPI levels, reflecting different urbanisation patterns, housing costs, and regional demand.

Regional CPI Journey

Explore how CPI changed across Malaysian states. Use interaction features to compare growth trajectories.

Spending Category Analysis

Inflation does not affect every spending category equally. Essential goods and services experienced stronger CPI growth, while other categories remained relatively stable.
